Tell me about it!

Portola Inn has a tuscan villa feel. The grounds are surrounded with a variety of trees, many being two hundred year old oaks, and there is a lovely garden to stroll and explore. We can't wait to host our wedding at this beautiful location!

As far as weather goes, the average temperature will be around 90F-95F at the hottest point in the day, and potentially 54F at the coolest during August. With the sun up during our ceremony, we would like to suggest wearing sunscreen, sunglasses and/or sun hats if you like.

Sounds amazing! What should I wear!?!

Semi-formal. It will be summer time, so dress for warm weather with light jackets for the evening if it cools off. The ceremony will be on a lovely grass lawn, so please keep this in mind when considering footwear!
